About Finance Finder

Hi! I'm Madelyn, the creator of Finance Finder. Finance Finder is on a mission to help people manage their finances in a smarter way. I also want this to be accessible to all people, so it is free and user-friendly! The user can input their needs, wants, jobs/salaries, and assets! It will calculate annual profit, annual income, annual debts, etc. And it will give them little tips too!
You can find more info by going to the presentation!
This is part of the AI4FinancialGood challenge by GWC - exploring how AI can be used to help people with their financial needs. I want to thank the staff, volunteers, and community at GWC for this opportunity. Enjoy Finance Finder!
